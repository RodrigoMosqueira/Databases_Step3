Welcome to OlympicDB!

OlympicDB is an interactive database designed for the Paris 2024 Summer Olympic Games. It aggregates data for the Athletes, Countries, Events, Venues, Coaches, and Officials who participated in the Olympics as well as their various relationships. Users can execute queries to answer common questions related to the Olympics and also insert their own data into the database.
